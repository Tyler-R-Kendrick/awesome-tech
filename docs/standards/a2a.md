# Agent2Agent Protocol (A2A)

An open standard for letting independent, opaque AI agents discover each other's capabilities, negotiate interaction, exchange information securely, and coordinate tasks across frameworks and vendors. It originated at Google (April 2025) and was donated to the Linux Foundation, which launched the A2A project in June 2025; the specification has reached v1.0.0.

## Footnotes

- Governed by a Technical Steering Committee including AWS, Cisco, Google, IBM Research, Microsoft, Salesforce, SAP, and ServiceNow.
- The `a2aproject` organization also hosts language SDKs such as a2a-python, a2a-js, a2a-go, and a2a-java.
